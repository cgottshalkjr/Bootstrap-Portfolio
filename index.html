About Me

I was born on the 27th of September in the year of our Lord 1985. The weather was dark and foreboding. A mighty hurricane had come crashing down on the fair city of Camden, New Jersey. Hurricane Gloria. My mother said if I were to be a maiden I would take on the name of the mighty Hurricane that brashed at the walls of the hospital. Thank the heavens I was an ugly baby boy.

Actually, nothing dark or foreboding came to fruition from that damned day. I grew into a normal young boy with simple aspirations. To one day be the greatest boxer the world had ever known. Well, that didn't happen. In fact I never stepped inside of a ring. :| All that malarky aside, I'm just a simple gentleman. I enjoy Boxing, am a Film nerd, and like to cook my tush off. Sichuan food is prob my fave. I've even read a book. If you want to get wrecked in Dominoes, get at me! If you want to go milling about in a different country, let's do it. Want to build something out of wood after watching a youtube video, what up?! Chopping wood, yep, I'm down. I'll fell a tree with the best of them!
